Shipping

Ground shipping charges for orders to be delivered within the contiguous 48 United States are based on the number of unique items in your order for orders totaling less than $35; orders of $35 or more ship free.
Residential orders within the 48 contiguous states will ship USPS Priority 1 business days of receipt of your order and will be delivered within 2â€“7 business days. If your postal address is different from your physical address, please provide both.

Shipping Charges for US Orders Being Shipped Outside of the 48 Contiguous States
Shipping charges for residential orders shipping to AK, HI, PR, VI, the Pacific Territories, and APO/FPO addresses, regardless of order total or weight, will require a freight quote. The shipping charge will show up as TBD (To Be Determined) within your order. We will contact you with a shipping quote within 24 hours of placing your order, and we will not ship your order without your approval. We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause. After approval, your order will be shipped via USPS Priority Mail.

Free Shipping
Merchandise orders totaling $35 or more ship freight-free via USPS Priority to the United States of America. Free Shipping does not apply to Expedited, Fast2Day, or Overnight Express Shipping. Oversized or overweight orders will ship via a freight line. In those cases, the free freight offer includes curbside delivery only. Additional services performed at the time of the delivery such as lift gate and inside delivery will incur additional charges. The average transit times will be extended for freight line deliveries and a delivery signature is required.
Exceptions: Additional shipping charges will be added to factory-direct shipments, international orders, and shipments to Alaska, Hawaii, Puerto Rico, and U.S. Territories. Shipments of certain stocked art materials will be made by truck and will be billed an additional charge.

Notes on Shipping Charges
Due to occasional carrier rate increases, the shipping rates shown above may differ from the rates that appear in previously published catalogs. Currently, we offer FREE SHIPPING for merchandise orders totaling $35 or more (with some exceptions). Shipping rates are subject to change without notice.
All shipping methods require a physical address. USPS Parcel Post is available only if you have no physical address (i.e., a PO Box), and USPS Ground charges will apply.
Any order shipping outside of the 48 states may incur additional shipping charges. Read more about International Shipping.

US Postal Service
Orders shipping to APO/FPO addresses, along with Alaska, Hawaii, Virgin Islands, Puerto Rico, and US Pacific Territories are shipped via the US Postal Service. Please allow additional time for delivery.
