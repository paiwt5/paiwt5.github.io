Blow Drip Technique


This technique allows the use of many Paiwt Color drops on a small amount of Paiwt Medium. For this example I will be using the Green, Orange, and Pink Introductory Paiwt Kit (also known as the Blow Drip Kit).


Start by pouring a small amount of Paiwt Medium onto a flat level surface (about a quarter size).


Drop one drop of Paiwt Color, here I am dropping Paiwt Pink.


Blow onto painting, spreading the Paiwt Color and Paiwt Medium.


Drop Paiwt Color or Expander. Here I am dropping Paiwt Green


Blow onto painting, then continue adding colors and blowing until desired design is reached. Here I have added a drop of Paiwt orange.


Awesome! You have made amazing artwork using a small amount of Paiwt Medium! Thank you for visiting, add to your bookmarks (Ctrl+D) and keep in touch.
