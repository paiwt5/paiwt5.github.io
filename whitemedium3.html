White Medium

Paiwt White Medium is a versatile formulation that retains its integrity forever. It dries in several hours and can be applied to any flat surface. As a base coat it spreads easily and once dry, multiple layers can be added if desired.


To use the dual-chamber dispensing bottles, simply loosen cap from dispensing chamber. Gently squeeze the bottle to fill measuring chamber with required amount of liquid. Remove cap from dispensing chamber and easily pour measured fluid from the chamber.
