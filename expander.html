Expander

By using Paiwt Expander, Paiwt offers the ability to create a powerful visual effect that will amplify your colors outwards in all directions while mixing the colors in the center creating a beautiful unique design that moves, dances, and grows each second. Apply one drop onto your layered colors and you will be dazzled by a unique display of moving and mixing colors; different color sequences produce different effects.


The expander helps the colors grow out if needed. When two or more colors are dropped onto or around each other, expander will mix the two+ colors together to form new colors. It can also be applied to single colors.

Paiwt Expander is a biodegradable, nontoxic, and all-natural formulation.
